Self-Driving Cars

Definition

A self-driving car, also known as an autonomous car, driverless car, robotaxi, robotic car, or robo-car, is a car capable of operating with reduced or no human input.

How They Work

Self-driving cars are responsible for all driving activities, such as perceiving the environment, monitoring important systems, and controlling the vehicle. This includes navigating from origin to destination without human intervention.

Waymo's Innovation

Waymo was the first to offer rides in self-driving taxis to the public in limited geographic areas.
