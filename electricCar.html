Electric Car Overview

Definition

An electric car is a passenger automobile powered by an electric traction motor, using electrical energy as its primary source of propulsion.

Advantages

Electric cars are quieter, more responsive, and have superior energy conversion efficiency. They generate no exhaust emissions and have a lower overall carbon footprint, even when considering the electricity generation process.

Efficiency

Due to the superior efficiency of electric motors, these vehicles produce less waste heat, reducing the need for large, complicated, and maintenance-prone engine cooling systems that are common in internal combustion engine vehicles.
